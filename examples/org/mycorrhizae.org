* Mycorrhizae

Mycorrhizae are symbiotic relationships between fungi and plant roots.

** Related
- [[id:112ef7cb-6ad3-4e7b-addf-bdb7ae986995][Plant Nutrition]], [[id:32723103-1772-4356-88d5-88e1b2655710][Soil Science]], [[id:581edafc-8add-4a7c-9ffe-f7fdeff7a0d5][Plant Defense]]

** Details

The fungal partner increases access to water and nutrients, while the plant supplies carbohydrates. These relationships connect soil ecology with plant health.
